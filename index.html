Councilman Eric Heffinger

Here to help

I love to help people.

Whether it's working with my students as a teacher, my time at home with my wife and kids, or my time on Medina's City Council, my life resolves around helping. The passion to work with and for people is why I am a city councilman.

If you need assistance from the city or just have a question, give me a call or email me. I will always get back to you personally.

It is an honor to work for you and Medina, and I hope I can count on your vote on November 5th.
